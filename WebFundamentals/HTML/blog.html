Wheel of Fortune

Wheel of Fortune is a American game show created by Merv Griffin. The show involves three contestants solve hangman puzzles and a big wheel. Contestants spin the wheel and guess letters to win cash and prizes. The wheel is filled with cash, prizes, and other unique elements. Some special elements include the Mystery wedge, Million Dollar wedge, Wild card, 1/2 Car, etc.
